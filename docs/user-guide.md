# User Guide

Specialty Coffee Roasters is a directory of Canadian micro-roasters that sell
beans online. Use it to find what's in stock right now, compare prices across
roasters, and keep a private log of beans you've tasted.

## Browsing

### By Roaster
The home page lists every roaster sorted by distance from your city. Click any
row for that roaster's bean catalog with current prices and stock state.

The location chip in the top-left is auto-detected from your IP. Click it to
override or clear (your selection is remembered in your browser only).

### By Bean
The "By Bean" tab is a flat list of every coffee across every roaster, with
filters for origin, process, roast level, varietal, and tasting notes. Use this
when you know what you want to try and want the cheapest source.

### Coffee detail
Click a bean to see:
- Full description, origin, process, roast, and tasting notes from the roaster
- All bag sizes with price, $/g, and ¢/g — sortable by best value
- Aggregate community rating from public tastings
- Recent public tastings with notes

## Accounts

Sign up with email + password, or use "Sign in with Google."

After you sign up we'll send a verification link. You can keep using the site
before verifying — you just won't get restock alerts until you do.

### Profile
Your display name (e.g. `alice_taster`) becomes your public profile URL:
`/u/alice_taster`. Pick something you're happy showing publicly — it can't be
changed by you (contact us if you need it changed).

## Tastings

On any coffee page, click "I tasted this" to log a tasting:
- **Rating** (optional, 1–5 stars internally stored as 1–10)
- **Notes** — what did it taste like, how did you brew it
- **Brew method** — V60, espresso, AeroPress, etc.
- **Date** — when you tasted it
- **Public toggle** — public tastings appear on the bean page, your profile,
  and at a permalink (`/t/123`) you can share. Private tastings are visible
  only to you.

You can edit or delete any of your own tastings. Deleting hides them from every
public surface immediately.

### Rate limit
New accounts can post up to 5 tastings per day for their first 7 days. After
that there's no limit (within reason).

## Wishlist

Click the heart on any bean card to add it to your private wishlist. Restock
alerts fire as a daily email digest if any wishlisted bean comes back in stock.

## Reporting content

Every public tasting has a tiny "Report" link. Use it if you see spam,
harassment, or anything inappropriate. Reports go to our moderation queue —
we don't auto-hide content on flag, every decision is human-reviewed.

## Email

We send transactional email only (verification, password reset, restock
alerts). No newsletters, no marketing.

To change your email or delete your account, contact us.
